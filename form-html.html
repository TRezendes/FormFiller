Submit Your Concern

Have you seen radical, divisive ideology or race-based discrimination creep into healthcare or medical education? Whether you’re a physician, patient, or student, we want to hear your story and help you fight back.

Q&A for Submitting Concerns

Yes. We recognize that confronting identity politics and calling out divisive ideology carries personal and professional risks. Protecting your privacy is our number-one priority.

We’re interested in stories that illustrate the radicalization taking place across healthcare. That includes “anti-racist” physician training; race-based restrictions on medical treatments; radical ideology in the medical school classroom; the weakening of standards in medical scholarship and training; race-based eligibility restrictions for medical school scholarships, fellowships, or programs, and more.

We’re happy to help. Please write to us if you’re willing to tell your story publicly and we’ll look for opportunities to support you.

Submitted concerns are often the first step in the process of exposing harmful practices in medicine. These tips will be used to submit document requests, file civil rights complaints, pursue litigation, or bring awareness to bad actors in healthcare. Please include as much information/evidence as possible so we can pursue your concern to the fullest extent.
